Jordbruksbyrån Åland सेंग अनुक्त हुन हुण Facebook मा समाचि है। New quarantine pest found in Finland The quarantine pest Tomato brown rugose fruit virus (ToBRFV) has been found for the first time in a Finnish crop in August 2022. The virus destroys the fruits and causes significant yield losses. Tomatoes and peppers affected by the ToBRFV virus are not harmful to eat.
