Xylella, new outbreak in the Bari area A new outbreak of Xylella has been identified in the Bari area. In Polignano a Mare 18 olive trees have been found to be infected in an area that has so far been unscathed. Coldiretti Puglia calls for a more incisive containment strategy and hopes for the allocation of new resources to deal with damage to the olive-growing, labor and tourism heritage that has reached almost 3 billion euros.
